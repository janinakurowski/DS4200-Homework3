Projects and interests

I like work that brings care to people and structure to ideas. Public health asks us to listen and data science asks us to test our claims. I like the place where these meet. I focus on careful questions, a clean plan for data, and a plain summary at the end. I enjoy the moment when a simple figure makes the result feel helpful. I try to write for a busy reader and I avoid jargon that adds no value. I also enjoy group work and I put care into naming, comments, and tests so the next step feels easy. I want to keep learning and to build things that make a difference.

Simple habits I try to follow

  • Write for a person who wants clarity
  • Choose charts that match the question
  • Document choices so future changes are easy
