Welcome to My Website

I enjoy the place where health science and data meet. I like to turn messy information into clear stories that help people decide what to do next. I practice this in classes and group projects. I focus on clean structure, careful naming, and writing that anyone on my team can read. I keep my work grounded in real needs and I aim for results that are easy to understand. I am learning more about evaluation so I can tell when something worked and why. I care about simple visuals that guide the eye and I keep the focus on the question. Outside of school I care for a few plants and I enjoy quiet reading time. This site collects simple ideas and small project updates. Thank you for visiting and I hope you find something useful here.
