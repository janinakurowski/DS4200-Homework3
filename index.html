Welcome to my website

Thanks for stopping by. Here, I share a bit about myself and the interests that shape my studies and work. I enjoy working on projects that link real community questions with careful analysis and clear communication. I like to figure out why patterns show up in the world and test those ideas with data and honest methods. I believe good work comes from clear thinking and respect for others. I prefer web pages that are simple and easy to read, and I value step-by-step notes that help new readers. My aim is to make complex topics a bit easier to understand.
